About Me

TRUBA INSTITUTE (https://www.trubainstitute.ac.in/)

Truba Group of Institutes at Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh, India is a group of two engineering institutes, Truba Institute of Engineering and Information Technology and Truba College of Science & Technology, one pharmacy institute, Truba Institute of Pharmacy and a management institute, Truba Institute of Science and Commerce. Truba Edu Society is also the parent society of Sage University(Truba) Indore. The name "Truba" is derived from the name of lord Tirupati Balaji. Truba Group of Institutes is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV), Bhopal, and offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in various fields of study, including engineering, management, pharmacy, computer applications, and education.
